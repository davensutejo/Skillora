Frequently Asked Questions

View All FAQs

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

How do I download course materials?

Course materials can be downloaded from within each course. Navigate to your course, and look for the download icon next to each resource. Some materials may not be available for download due to copyright restrictions.

What happens when I complete a course?

When you complete all required assignments and achieve a passing grade, you'll receive a completion certificate. Your progress will be updated on your dashboard, and the course will be added to your completed courses list.

How do I get a refund?

Refund policies vary by course. Generally, you can request a refund within 7 days of purchase if you've completed less than 25% of the course. To request a refund, go to your account settings, select "Billing" and click on "Request Refund" next to the course.

Can I transfer my course to someone else?

Courses are non-transferable between accounts. Each subscription or course purchase is tied to the account that made the purchase. If you need to arrange access for someone else, they will need to create their own account and purchase the course separately.
